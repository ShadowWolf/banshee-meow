Wedding Information

Saint Paul Hotel

350 Market St Saint Paul, MN 55102

August 18th, 4:30 - Midnight


With respect, we would like our special day to be an adult only occasion.

We recommend arriving fifteen minutes before the ceremony time begins.


Our wedding will be fully indoors (with air conditioning!) at a historical boutique hotel, so dressy/wedding casual is encouraged. Be sure to wear your dancing shoes!

We will be offering a full selection of cocktails, beer, and wine for a portion of the night and will switch to a cash bar for those who wish to partake later into the night.

 

Ceremony Directions

The ceremony and reception will be hosted in the lower level of the Saint Paul Hotel.

If you’re unsure of where to go, there will be an electronic sign across from the Saint Paul Grill that will guide you. There will also be a greeter – either Mary Jo or someone from her team – directing people near the main entrance.

  • From the main entrance (located on Market Street): take a left and go down the grand staircases.
  • From the side entrance (located on 5th and St. Peter Street): take a right, pass the check-in/concierge, and turn right to the grand staircase.
  • From the skyway: go down the flight of stairs from the skyway, pass the conference rooms and into the lobby. Walk across the lobby, pass the check-in/concierge, and take a right to the grand staircase.

Directions to The Saint Paul Hotel

Parking Cost: $27/day valet (unlimited in and out) or $16/day to self-park (per entry).

From Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport (MSP)

(Terminal 1) From Glumack Dr, keep a left at the fork and merge onto MN-5 E. Stay on MN-5 E for about 1.5 miles.

(Terminal 2) From 34th Ave S, use the left two lanes to turn left and merge onto MN-5 E. Stay on MN-5 E for about 2.5 miles.

Follow MN-5 E until you cross the Mississippi River. Take the exit for Shepard Rd. Be sure to hang a right as you go up the off-ramp as this exit feeds into two other streets.

Take a left onto Shepard Rd to head east to Saint Paul. Stay on Shepard Rd for about 5 miles.

Take a left onto Eagle Parkway and drive up the hill.

Hook a right onto Exchange Street; immediately turn right onto Kellogg Boulevard.

Take a left onto Market Street. The Saint Paul Hotel will be on your left. You can either valet your car or park in the Landmark Towers Parking Ramp (entrance will be right before the hotel on the right).

From I-94 (Wisconsin)

Stay on I-94 W for about 16 miles until you reach downtown Saint Paul.

Use the left lane to take exit 242D for 6th Street.

When you reach the intersection with St. Peter Street, use the 2nd from the right lane to stay on W 6th St.

Turn left at the first cross street onto N Market St. The Saint Paul Hotel will be on your left. You can either valet your car or park in the Landmark Towers Parking Ramp (entrance will be right after the hotel on the left).

Accommodations

There is a guest code for those staying in the Saint Paul Hotel, where the ceremony and reception will be. Downtown St. Paul also offers several hotel options in the area that range in budget – all within walking distance. We recommend staying at the St. Paul Hotel or coordinating your own transportation after the event. We will not be coordinating or providing transportation to or from the hotel, but we will be there starting on Friday!

We recommend using transportation services such as Uber and Lyft to get around. Saint Paul Hotel offers a shuttle to the airport (but cannot pick up from the airport).

Travel Information

Minneapolis/Saint Paul is home to an amazing public transportation system. We recommend using their the website to find the best way to get where want to go: Metro Transit Website

If you are looking for food, entertainment, or sight seeing places then we recommend checking out some of these places

  • Minnehaha Regional Park (one of the nicest parks in the area)
  • Blue Door Pub (Home of the Blucy - worth checking out!)
  • Mill City Museum (former home of the world's largest flour mill)
  • Harriet Island on the Mississippi River
  • The Happy Gnome (amazing beer and bloody mary selection)
  • Hell's Kitchen (Not related to Gordon Ramsay but still about as loud and awesome)
  • Forepaughs (our favorite happy hour place)
  • Alexis Bailly Vineyard (our favorite winery – 35 minutes southeast of the Twin Cities)
