Travel Information

Minneapolis/St Paul is home to an amazing public transportation system. We recommend using their the website to find the best way to get where want to go: Metro Transit Website

If you are looking for food, entertainment, or sight seeing places, we recommend checking out some of these places

  • Minnehaha Regional Park (one of the nicest parks in the area)
  • Blue Door Pub (Home of the Blucy - worth checking out!)
  • Mill City Museum (former home of the world's largest flour mill)
  • Harriet Island on the Mississippi River
  • The Happy Gnome (amazing beer and bloody mary selection)
  • Hell's Kitchen (Not related to Gordon Ramsay but still about as loud and awesome)
  • Forepaughs (our favorite happy hour place)
  • Alexis Bailly Vineyard (our favorite winery – 35 minutes southeast of the Twin Cities)
